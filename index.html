Need Answers?

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't get results?

We offer a revise-or-refund guarantee. If we don't deliver agreed-upon results within the first 60 days, we'll either revise our approach at no extra cost or refund your investment. Our contracts are completely risk-free with no long-term commitments.

How quickly can I see leads/sales?

Most campaigns go live within 72 hours of onboarding. You'll typically start seeing initial results within 7-14 days, with optimization cycles every 48 hours. Full-funnel impact usually becomes evident within 30-45 days as we gather sufficient data.

Why choose performance marketing?

Traditional agencies charge retainers regardless of results. Performance marketing aligns our incentives with your success—we only succeed when you do. This model eliminates wasted spend and focuses every dollar on measurable outcomes.

Can you work with my industry?

We specialize in e-commerce, SaaS, B2B services, local businesses, healthcare, finance, and professional services. Even if your industry is highly regulated (like healthcare or finance), we have compliant strategies that deliver results.

How do you ensure lead quality?

We implement multi-layer validation including AI scoring, manual verification, and custom qualification workflows. For PPL campaigns, we establish clear lead criteria upfront and only count leads that meet all requirements.

What's your pricing model?

We offer three flexible options: 1) Pay-per-lead (fixed rate per qualified lead), 2) Pay-per-sale (percentage of revenue), or 3) Hybrid model (lower management fee + performance bonus). Pricing varies by industry and campaign complexity.
